Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico.

It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Lime
  • Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  2. Step 2

    3. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
